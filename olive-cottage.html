Welcome

A home away from home, not a hotel lobby.

Olive Cottage has always worked on a simple idea: guests should feel looked after, not processed. It's a small, privately owned guest house — quiet, comfortable and personal, built for people who want a proper night's rest rather than a lobby full of strangers.

We're not trying to be the biggest name in Bloemfontein. We're trying to be the guest house people come back to — whether that's a business trip, a visit to family at UFS, or simply a stop on the way through on the N1.

  • Privately owned and personally run
  • Quiet residential setting in Dan Pienaar
  • Secure, gated parking on site
