Welcome

Run by Chris & Sophia — it shows.

Bell Guesthouse sits quietly at the edge of Somerset East, with the Boschberg rising behind the garden. Chris and Sophia Wilken run it themselves, and it's the kind of small, personal touches — a tidy garden, a full kitchen ready to use, milk already in the fridge — that guests tend to mention first.

It's self-catering at heart, so you can cook, relax on the veranda, or let us handle breakfast and dinner by arrangement. Either way, it's a quiet, comfortable base for exploring the town, the Boschberg trails, or simply doing nothing at all.

  • Personally run by the owners, Chris & Sophia Wilken
  • Private entrance, fully self-catering
  • Landscaped garden with mountain views
